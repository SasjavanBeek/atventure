#F*ckItList

~ Be spontaneous! ~

Top 10 things you need to do in your lifetime

There are so many possibilities in life. More than we can ever imagine. So what are you going to do? Just sit and wait? No, YOU take action. Do anything your heart wants to, no matter how weird it sounds. You only live ones. Here's 10 you need to do in your lifetime.

1. Run a half marathon

That is just over 13 miles. In our life we walk 4 orbits around the whole earth, that is about 110,000 miles. Those 13 miles are nothing. Running a half marathon is a cool experience and you will also train your perseverance to use it in the rest of your adventures.

2. Jump out of an airplane (with a parachute)

No one can take this experience away from you. If you've ever been on an airplane, you know how beautiful the view is up there. But trust me, the sightseeing is even better when you jump out of the plane. Of course with guidance :)

3. Volunteer

Volunteer for a good project or contribute to a positive change in other people's lives. Not only that. You will develop great skills and you will meet a lot of new friends.

4. Have a cup of coffee with a stranger

Meeting new people can be hard. Especially when you are following your daily routine over and over again. Starting a conversation with a stranger will lead to moments you will never forget. You will also build a lot of self-confidence and you will learn a lot from other people, their stories, their behaviors, or their experiences. Nobody is the same and that's wonderful. You might build up beautiful friendships or you might meet your soulmate. You will never know. Try it, it's fun!

5. Write a book and publish it

Everyone has a story to tell. So do you. Writing can help you process better. Your story can help, teach, and entertain others. You don't have to write your story. Just write a story. A story you made up or an experience you had. Other people will love to read it. So why are your stories not out there yet? There's no reason to wait.

6. Go for a roadtrip

After my road trip from San Francisco to Las Vegas with Rick and Leonard in 2011 I still have a wish to have a nice trip. This can also be around Europe. Check it off your bucket list!

7. Pack your bags and book a mystery flight

Go see the world! Go with the flow and don't plan anything. This will lead you to the most beautiful places and memories. You can go with friends or you can go alone. Roadtripping by yourself will make you do things you never believed you could. So go for it, pack your backs and see where it goes (you go:).

8. Be in a film or commercial

Even if you're just an extra and you're only on screen for 3 seconds. Even if you only get to say one sentence you can honestly say, you are in that commercial or film.

9. Play hide and seek in de Ikea or another megastore

F*ck it! Just do it. Let the child in you come out again! These are the moments we live for.

10. Go skydiving

As a child, everyone dreams of learning to fly. Here's your chance. Grab it and feel as light as a feather.
