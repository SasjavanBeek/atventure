#F*ckItList

Be spontaneous!

Top 10 things you need to do in your lifetime

There are so many possibilities in life. More than we can ever imagine. So what are you going to do? Just sit and wait? No, you take action. Do anything your heart wants to, no matter how weird it sounds. You only live ones.

1. Run a half marathon

That is just over 21 kilometers. In my current condition, I will probably give up after 200 meters. I get tired already when I touch it think.

2. Jump out of an airplane (with a parachute)

With my current weight may be an idea to finish this assignment to accomplish. Could go wrong. Or at least after running out of the half marathon.

3. Volunteer

Volunteer for a good project or contribute to a positive change in other people's lives.

4. Have a cup of coffee with a stranger

Of course, I pay the bill. Put it on your bucket list!

5. Write a book and publish it

After a few nice comments on my blogs during my holiday in Bali I want to do my very best to make this already long dream come true come. A start has now been made and the first few chapters are on paper.

6. Go for a roadtrip

After my road trip from San Francisco to Las Vegas with Rick and Leonard in 2011 I still have a wish to have a nice trip. This can also be around Europe. Check it off your bucket list!

7. Pack your bags and book a mystery flight

Saw that this is now possible through KLM. With your suitcase with swimming trunks and sunglasses being dropped in Iceland seems like a good idea to me.

8. Be in a film or commercial

Even if I'm just an extra and I'm only on screen for 3 seconds. Like this played my brother in the movie "Snuf de Hond" and he also had 1 whole sentence that he could say. I want that too!

9. Play hide and seek in de Ikea or another megastore

The child in me comes out again!

10. Go skydiving

A bit cliche, but looks really cool!
